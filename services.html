Services

Hearing Evaluations

A Hearing Evaluation is an in-depth assessment of your hearing conducted by an Audiologist. During the assessment, certain tests will be conducted to test different parts of your hearing system. During the tests, the nature and degree of your hearing will be determined and recommendations will be made depending on the results.

Hearing Screening

Hearing Screening is a quick test to determine if the patient has normal hearing or a hearing loss. The degree/severity isn’t tested.

Wax Removal

We remove wax depending on the depth and consistency of the wax.

Hearing Aids

There are many styles of hearing aids – behind-the-ear, in-the-ear, receiver-in-the-canal etc. The degree of your hearing loss is considered when choosing a specific style of hearing aid. Hearing aids are also available in different performance levels and colours.

Hearing Aid Fitting Appointment

During this appointment, the audiologist will teach you how to use and care for your new instruments. Your hearing aids will also be Verified using Verification Software to ensure that the hearing aids provide the correct amplification at all the different frequencies in your hearing spectrum. Some fine tuning adjustments may be needed and follow-up appointments will be scheduled.
